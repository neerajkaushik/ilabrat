The persons having no recognized and approved qualifications , having little knowledge about the indigenous medicines , are becoming medical practitioners and playing with the lives of thousands and millions of people . Some time such quacks commit blunders and precious lives are lost . 20 ) The government had been vigilant all along to stop such quackery . A number of unqualified , untrained quacks are posing a great risk to the entire society and playing with the lives of people without having the requisite training and education in the science from approved institutions . The Travancore-Cochin Medical Practitioners Act , 1953 as well as the Indian Medicine Central Council Act , 1970 were also enacted on the similar lines .

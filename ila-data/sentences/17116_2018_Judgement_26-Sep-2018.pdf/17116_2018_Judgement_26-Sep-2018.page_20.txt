However , if that application fails , the appellant can be permitted to reopen or revive the challenge to the issues raised in Election Application No. 6 of 2017 for dismissal of the election petition under Order VII Rule 11 ( a ) & ( d ) of the CPC , on the ground that the election petition does not disclose a cause of action or that it is barred by law . Further , both such special leave petitions can be heard together . All questions in that regard are kept open , to be decided appropriately if and when occasion arises .

Explanation 2 . — The making of a false document in the name of a fictitious person , intending it to be believed that the document was made by a real person , or in the name of a deceased person , intending it to be believed that the document was made by the person in his lifetime , may amount to forgery . Therefore , we can safely deduce that Section 464 defines one of the ingredients of forgery i.e. , making of a false document .

In order to establish the offence under Section 8 of the Prevention of Corruption Act it must be proved : ( 1 ) That the accused accepted or obtained , or agreed to accept , or attempted to obtain , from someone ; ( ii ) For himself or for some other person ; ( ii ) Any gratification whatever ; ( iv ) As a motive or reward for inducing by corrupt or illegal means any ‘ public servant ’ to do or forbear to do any official act or to show favour or render any service to any of the _ persons specified in the section . 6 .

It was held that a Physician would not assure the patient of full recovery in every case . A surgeon can not and does not guarantee that the result of surgery would invariably be beneficial , much less to the extent of 100 % for the person operated on . The only assurance which such a professional can give or can be understood to have given by implication is that he is possessed of the requisite skill in that branch of profession which he is practicing and while undertaking the performance of the task entrusted to him he would be exercising his skill with reasonable competence .

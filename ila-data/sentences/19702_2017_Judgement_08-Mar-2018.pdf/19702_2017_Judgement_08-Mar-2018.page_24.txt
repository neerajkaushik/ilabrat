The role of the Court is to see that the detenue is produced before it , find out about his/her independent choice and see to it that the person is released from illegal restraint . The issue will be a different one when the detention is not illegal . What is seminal is to remember that the song of liberty is sung with sincerity and the choice of an individual is appositely respected and conferred its esteemed status as the Constitution guarantees . It is so as the expression of choice is a fundamental right under Articles 19 and 21 of the Constitution , if the said choice does not transgress any valid legal framework .

Since all the three agreements of Rishabh with Juwi India and Astonfield had the purpose of commissioning the Photovoltaic Solar Plant project at Dongri , Raksa , District Jhansi , Uttar Pradesh , the High Court was not right in saying that the Sale and Purchase Agreement ( 05.03.2012 ) is the main agreement . The High Court , in our view , erred in not keeping in view the various clauses in all the three agreements which make them as an integral part of the principal agreement namely Equipment Lease Agreement ( 14.03.2012 ) and the impugned order of the High Court can not be sustained .

The employer is also entitled to prove it otherwise against the employee , namely , that the employee was gainfully employed during the relevant period and hence not entitled to claim any back wages . Initial burden is , however , on the employee . 13 . In some cases , the Court may decline to award the back wages in its entirety whereas in some cases , it may award partial depending upon the facts of each case by exercising its judicial discretion in the light of the facts and evidence .

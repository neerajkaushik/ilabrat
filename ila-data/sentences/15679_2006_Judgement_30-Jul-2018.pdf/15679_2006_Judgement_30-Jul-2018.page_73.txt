The notification in this case was issued under Rule 8 of the Central Excise Rules and should be read along with the Act . The notification must be read as a whole in the context of the other relevant provisions . When a notification is issued in accordance with power conferred by the statute , it has statutory force and validity and , therefore , the exemption under the notification is as if it were contained in the Act itself . See in this connection the observations of this Court in Orient Weaving Mills ( P ) Ltd. v. Union of India , 1962 Supp 3 SCR 481 = AIR 1963 SC 98 . See also Kailash Nath v. State of U-P. , AIR 1957 SC 790 .

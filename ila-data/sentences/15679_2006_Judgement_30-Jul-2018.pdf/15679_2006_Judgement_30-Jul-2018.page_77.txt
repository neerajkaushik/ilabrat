The next authority , which needs to be referred is the case in Mangalore Chemicals ( supra ) . As we have already made reference to the same earlier , repetition of the same is not necessary . From the above decisions , the following position of law would , therefore , clear . Exemptions from taxation have tendency to increase the burden on the other unexempted class of tax payers . A person claiming exemption , therefore , has to establish that his case squarely falls within the exemption notification , and while doing so , a notification should be construed against the subject in case of ambiguity .

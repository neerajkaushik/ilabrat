PART N 6 ) Hindu women constitute a ‘ section or class ’ of Hindus under clauses ( b ) and ( c ) of Section 2 of the 1965 Act . Rule 3 ( b ) of the 1965 Rules enforces a custom contrary to Section 3 of the 1965 Act . This directly offends the right of temple entry established by Section 3 . Rule 3 ( b ) is ultra vires the 1965 Act .

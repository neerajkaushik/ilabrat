As per clause ( a ) of Section 2 , the term ' Hindu ' includes a person professing Buddhist , Sikh or Jaina religion . The word ‘ person ' occurring in this clause , for the pure and simple reason of logic , must include all genders . Clause ( c ) defines ' section or class ' as any division , sub-division , caste , sub-caste , sect or denomination whatsoever . Nowhere the definition of section or class suggests being limited to male division , sub-division , caste and so forth . 129 .

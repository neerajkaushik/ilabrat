PART K The object of the Act is to enable the entry of all sections and classes of Hindus into temples dedicated to , or for the benefit of or used by any section or class of Hindus . The Act recognizes the rights of all sections and classes of Hindus to enter places of public worship and their right to offer prayers . The law was enacted to remedy centuries of discrimination and is an emanation of Article 25 ( 2 ) ( b ) of the Constitution . The broad and liberal object of the Act can not be shackled by the exclusion of women . Rule 3 ( b ) is ultra vires .

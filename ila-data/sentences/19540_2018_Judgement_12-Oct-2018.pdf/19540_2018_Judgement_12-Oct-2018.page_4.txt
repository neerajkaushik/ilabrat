Urban Improvement Trust , Kota . 4 . Section 10 ( 20 ) has been amended by Finance Act , 2002 w.e.f. 01.04.2003 . A Notice under Section 142 ( 1 ) of the Income Tax Act , 1961 ( hereinafter referred to as “ I.T. Act ” ) was issued dated 01.08.2005 requiring the assessee to file a return for the assessment year 20032004 . A reply was submitted on behalf of the assessee that Urban Improvement Trust - the assessee is a municipality within the meaning of Article 243P of the Constitution of India , hence it is not required to file an income tax return . Assessing Officer passed an assessment order dated 28.03.2006 rejecting the contention of the assessee that its income is exempted under Section 10 ( 20 ) . An appeal was filed by the assessee before the Commissioner ( Appeals ) . Commissioner ( Appeals ) passed an order on 10.02.2010 holding that assessee is a local authority within the meaning of Section 10 ( 20 ) of the I.T. Act .

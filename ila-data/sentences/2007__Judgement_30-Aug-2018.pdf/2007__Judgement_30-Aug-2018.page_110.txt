Scheduled Caste/Scheduled Tribe in State ' A ' . The expressions " in relation to that State or Union Territory " and " for the purpose of this Constitution " used in Articles 341 and 342 of the Constitution of India are to be meaningfully interpreted . A given caste or tribe can be a Scheduled Caste or a Scheduled Tribe in relation to that State or Union Territory for which it is specified . Thus , the person notified as a Scheduled Caste in State ' A ’ can not claim the same status in another State on the basis that he was declared Scheduled Caste in State ' A ’ .

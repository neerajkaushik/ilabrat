Therefore , the members of the All India Services are common to the Union and the States and they serve , by turn , both the Union and State Governments . The members of these services although recruited by the Centre their services are placed under various State cadres . “ It is evident from Article 312 ( 1 ) that the members of the AIS are common to the Union and the States . Curiously , however , there is a cadre for the Indian Administrative Service only in the States , according to the Indian Administrative Service ( Cadre ) Rules , 1954 and there is no separate cadre for members of this service in the Government of India .

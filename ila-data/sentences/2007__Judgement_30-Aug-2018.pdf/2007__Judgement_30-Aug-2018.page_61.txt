Members of these services are allocated a cadre under a State or Union Territory and they serve the Union as well as the State , whichever is allotted to them . Thus their services are All India Services . Their recruitment is by the Union Public Service Commission and as they serve the Union as well as the States , their recruitment is on pan India basis .

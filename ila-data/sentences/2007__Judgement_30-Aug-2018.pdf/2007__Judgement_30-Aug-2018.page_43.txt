Any expansion/deletion of the list of Scheduled Castes/Scheduled Tribes by any authority except Parliament would be against the constitutional mandate under Articles 341 and 342 of the Constitution of India . 33 . Article 16 ( 4 ) is an enabling provision . It enables the State to provide to backward classes including Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes reservation in appointments to public services . Such reservation is to be provided on the basis of quantifiable data indicating the adequacy or inadequacy , as may be , of the representation of such classes in Government service . The data which is the basis of the satisfaction of the State being verifiable , is open to judicial scrutiny on the limited ground of relevance of the circumstances on which the satisfaction is moulded .

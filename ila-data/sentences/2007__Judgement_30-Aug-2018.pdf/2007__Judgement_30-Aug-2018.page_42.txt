However , there would be no infraction of clause ( 4 ) of Article 16 if a Union Territory by virtue of its peculiar position being governed by the President as laid down in Article 239 extends the benefit of reservation even to such migrant Scheduled Castes or Scheduled Tribes who are not mentioned in the Schedule to the Presidential Order issued for such Union Territory . The UT of Pondicherry having adopted a policy of the Central Government whereunder all Scheduled Castes or Scheduled Tribes , irrespective of their State are eligible for posts which are reserved for SC/ST candidates , no legal infirmity can be ascribed to such a policy and the same can not be held to be contrary to any provision of law .

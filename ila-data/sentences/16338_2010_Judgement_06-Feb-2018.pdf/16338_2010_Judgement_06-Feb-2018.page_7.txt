It was contended that if the respondents ( landowners ) were aggrieved by the order of the Collector , which declined to award the interest on the compensation , their appropriate remedy was to seek a reference to the Civil Court as provided under Section 28A ( 3 ) of the Act , it being a statutory remedy available to them under the Act but not in filing the writ petitions under Article 226 of the Constitution . 14 . The Single Judge by order dated 13.04.1999 , overruled the preliminary objection , allowed the writ petitions on merits and awarded the interest as claimed by the respondents . The Union of India , being aggrieved by the order of the Single Judge , filed intra court appeals before the Division Bench .

In sub clause ( b ) expression used is “ established by or under a Central , State or Provincial Act ” , in sub clause ( c ) the expression used is “ established under the Life Insurance Corporation Act ” and in sub clause ( d ) expression used is “ established under the Unit Trust of India Act ” . The Section thus uses both the expressions “ by or under ” . The expression established by or under an Act have come for consideration before this Court on several occasions . In this context , it shall be useful to refer to few judgments of this Court . In Sukhdev Singh ( supra ) , the Court had occasion to consider the status of company incorporated under the Companies Act . The Court held that Company incorporated is not a Company created by the Companies Act . In paragraph No. 25 following was held : MS sneak company incorporated under the Companies Act is not created by the Companies Act but comes into existence in accordance with the provisions of the Act . It is not a statutory body because it is not created by the statute . It is a body created in accordance with the provisions of the statute . ” 20 .

The case on behalf of the original plaintiff that the agreement to sell dated 20.4.1987 was a complete sale and it was a Sale deed can not be accepted for the simple reason that the said agreement / agreement to sell dated 20.4.1987 was not a registered one and even the same was not on the proper stamp duty and , therefore , as such , the same is not admitted in evidence and the same can be used only for the collateral purpose . Therefore , the agreement to sell dated 20.4.1987 is rightly considered to be the agreement to sell only . Even considering the submissions made by the learned counsel appearing on behalf of the plaintiff and even considering the averments made in the agreement to sell dated 20.4.1987 , the same can be said to be an agreement to sell only , as even the title deed / sale deed in favour of the original defendant no . 1 was executed in the year 1994 .

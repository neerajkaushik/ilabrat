As is well - known Government servants hold office during the pleasure of the President or the Governor , as the case may be , under Article 310 of the Constitution . However , the pleasure doctrine under Article 310 is limited by Article 311(2 ) . It is clear that the services of a permanent Government servant can not be terminated except in accordance with the rules made under Article 309 subject to Article 311(2 ) of the Constitution and the Fundamental Rights . It is also wellsettled that even a temporary Government servant or a probationer can not be dismissed or removed or reduced in rank except in accordance with Article 311(2 ) . The above doctrine of pleasure is invoked by the Government in the public interest after a Government servant attains the age of 50 years or has completed 25 years of service .

Section 1 of the Indian Stamp Act , 1899 as it originally stood , reads as follows : “ 1 . Short title , extent , and commencement.—(1 ) This Act may be called the Indian Stamp Act , 1899 . ( 2 ) It extends to the whole of British India inclusive of Upper Burma , British Baluchistan , the Santal Parganas , and the Pargana of Spiti ; and ( 3 ) It shall come into force on the first day of July 1899 . ” Section 2(14 ) defines “ instrument ” as follows : “ 2 . Definitions . — XXX XXX XXX ( 14 ) “ Instrument ” includes every document by which any right or liability is or purports to be created , transferred , limited , extended , extinguished , or recorded : ” “ Instruments chargeable with duty ” are dealt with by Section 3 .

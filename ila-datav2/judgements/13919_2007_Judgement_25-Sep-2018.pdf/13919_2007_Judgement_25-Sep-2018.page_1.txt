Le In this hotly contested appeal , the surviving issue is solely with regard to the claims made by the 29 canteen workers whether they are represented through the union or _ otherwise , for absorption . 2 » Having heard the learned counsel appearing for the Bank and having heard the learned Senior counsel appearing for the workmen and having regard the various orders passed by this Court , we are of the considered view that this is a fit case to invoke our jurisdiction under Article 142 of the Constitution of India and give a quietus to the dispute . 3 . In the peculiar facts and circumstances of this case , we direct the appellant — Punjab National Bank to appoint 29 canteen workers forthwith on the following conditions :   ( i ) They shall be paid half wages of the Class - IV employees or prevailing minimum wages in the State , whichever is meee ( ai ) All other benefits which have been granted to the 23 canteen workers already appointed will also be extended to the 29 workers covered by this order .

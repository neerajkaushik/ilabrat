One must also remember that compensation can be paid only once and not time and again . 6 . The traditional view was that while assessing compensation , the Court should assess the loss of income caused to the claimants by the death of the deceased and balance it with the benefits which may have accrued on account of the death of the deceased . However , even when this traditional view was being followed , it was a well settled position of law that the tort - feasor can not take benefit of the munificence or gratuity of others . 7 .

The High Court , in our view , failed to examine the main question as to whether a payment of Rs.110/- was in the nature of “ wages ” or its component within the meaning of Section 2(y ) of the Act . Without deciding this question , the High Court held that such amount could be adjusted against the payment made by the employer ( respondent ) to the appellant under “ leave encashment ” . In our opinion , it was not the correct approach . 39 ) In the light of afore - mentioned reasons , though we uphold the conclusion of the High Court but do not agree to the reasoning on which such conclusion is based .

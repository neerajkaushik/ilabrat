Article 239A was inserted by the Constitution ( Fourteenth Amendment ) Act , 1962 . Article 239A of the Constitution of India empowers the Parliament to create by law legislatures and Council of Ministers in the then Union Territories of Himachal Pradesh , Manipur , Goa , Daman & Diu and Pondicherry . Arunachal Pradesh and Mizoram were added later . With Himachal Pradesh , Manipur , Tripura , Goa and Mizoram all becoming full - fledged States , the only Union Territory left under Article 239A is Pondicherry . Now , Union Territory of Pondicherry ( Puducherry ) also has a legislature and Council of Ministers . 32 .

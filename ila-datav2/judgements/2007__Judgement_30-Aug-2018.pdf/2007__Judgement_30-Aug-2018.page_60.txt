There are many State Cadres e.g. Bihar Cadre , Rajasthan Cadre and Joint Cadres like AGMUT ( for Arunachal Pradesh + Goa + Mizoram + the 7 Union Territories ) . These officers remain in the allotted Cadres till they retire . They are not normally to be transferred from one State Cadre to another State Cadre though they have the accountability to serve both under the State and the Centre . 46 .

The Tribunal also noted that the maruti car was purchased by Manjeet Singh about 1 - 1 % years before the accident but the same was not transferred in his name nor was it insured . Taking an overall view of the matter , the Tribunal dismissed the claim petition vide judgment dated 10 " December , 2014 . 6 . The appellants carried the matter in appeal before the High Court of Uttarakhand at Nainital . The High Court summarily dismissed the appeal by reiterating the finding recorded by the Tribunal that the evidence clearly indicated that the driver of the maruti car himself was negligent in driving his vehicle and had failed to keep sufficient distance between the two vehicles running in the same direction .

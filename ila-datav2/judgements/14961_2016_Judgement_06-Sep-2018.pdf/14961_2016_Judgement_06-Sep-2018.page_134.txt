Explanation 2 to Section 375 IPC gives the definition of consent for the purpose of Section 375 to the effect that consent means an unequivocal voluntary agreement by the woman through words , gestures or any form of verbal or non - verbal communication whereby she communicates her willingness to participate in any of the sexual acts described in the former part of Section 375 IPC . 210 .

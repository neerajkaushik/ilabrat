The immobility due to fear corrodes the desire to express one ’s own sexual orientation as a consequence of which the body with flesh and bones feels itself caged and a sense of fear gradually converts itself into a skeleton sans spirit . 107 . The question of freedom of choosing a partner is reflective from a catena of recent judgments of this Court such as Shafin Jahan ( supra ) wherein the Court held that a person who has come of age and has the capability to think on his / her own has a right to choose his / her life partner . It is apposite to reproduce some of the observations made by the Court which are to the following effect : “ It is obligatory to state here that expression of choice in accord with law is acceptance of individual identity . Curtailment of that expression and the ultimate action emanating therefrom on the conceptual structuralism of obeisance to the societal will destroy the individualistic entity of a person . The social values and morals have their space but they are not above the constitutionally guaranteed freedom . The said freedom is both a constitutional and a human right . Deprivation of that freedom which is ingrained in choice on the plea of faith is impermissible . ” 108 .

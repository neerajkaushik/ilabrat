A contention was also advanced that the interpretation placed in Suresh Kumar ( supra ) upon Article 21 of the Constitution is extremely narrow and , in fact , the Court has been basically guided by Article 14 of the Constitution . Reliance was placed on the pronouncement in NALSA case wherein this Court had emphasized on “ gender identity and sexual orientation ” .

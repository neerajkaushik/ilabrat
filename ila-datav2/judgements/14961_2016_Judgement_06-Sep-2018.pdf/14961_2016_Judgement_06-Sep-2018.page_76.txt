" ” ' This statement of Dr. Ambedkar underscores that constitutional morality is not a natural forte for our country for the simple reason that our country had attained freedom after a long period of colonial rule and , therefore , constitutional morality at the time when the Constituent Assembly was set up was an alien notion . However , the strengthening of constitutional morality in contemporary India remains a duty of the organs of the State including the Judiciary . 115 .

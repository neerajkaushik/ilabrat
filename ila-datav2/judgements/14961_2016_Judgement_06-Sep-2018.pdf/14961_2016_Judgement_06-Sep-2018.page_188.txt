Chief Justice Burger , concurring , again relied heavily on ‘ ancient roots ’ , stating that throughout the history of western civilization , homosexual sodomy was outlawed in the Judeo - Christian tradition , which the Georgia legislature could well follow . Justice Powell , concurring with the majority , found that to imprison a person upto 20 years for a _ single , private , consensual act of sodomy within the home would be a cruel and unusual punishment within the meaning of the Eighth Amendment . However , since no trial had taken place on the facts , and since the respondent did not raise any such Eighth Amendment issue , Justice Powell concurred with the majority . 32 .

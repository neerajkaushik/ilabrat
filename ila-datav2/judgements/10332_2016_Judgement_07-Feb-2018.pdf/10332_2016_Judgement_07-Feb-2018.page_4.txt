XXV of 1997 ) ( for short , Contempt of Courts Act ) was enacted to define and limit the power of certain courts in Punishing contempt of courts and _ to regulate their procedure in relation thereto . However , Section 10 of the Contempt of Courts Act vests with the High Court the power to punish contempts of subordinate courts .

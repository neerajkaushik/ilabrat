If in the opinion of a State it is necessary to extend the benefit of reservation to a class / category of Scheduled Castes / Scheduled Tribes beyond those specified in the Lists for that particular State , constitutional discipline would require the State to make its views in the matter prevail with the central authority so as to enable an appropriate parliamentary exercise to be made by an amendment of the Lists of Scheduled Castes / Scheduled Tribes for that particular State .

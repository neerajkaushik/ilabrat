113 54 . Delhi , Andaman and Nicobar Islands Police Services ( DANIPS ) are recruited directly through centralised civil services examination conducted by UPSC . DANIPS are posted at Delhi and other Union Territories — Andaman and Nicobar Islands , Lakshadweep Islands , Daman and Diu and Dadar and Nagar Haveli . The cadre strength is controlled by the Ministry of Home Affairs , Government of India . Two - thirds of DANIPS are filled by direct recruitment and remaining are promoted from non - gazetted police officers of Union Territory of Delhi and other Union Territories . Since , DANIPS officers are recruited through a centralised recruitment conducted through UPSC , applications are invited from candidates from across the country including reserved candidates of Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes of all the States and Union Territories . Thus , up to Group ‘ B ’ ( Gazetted ) of Central Civil Services including the Group ‘ B ’ ( Gazetted ) services of Union Territories , there is Centralised Civil Services Examination conducted by UPSC with PAN INDIA reservation . 55 .

It is important to bear in mind that it is by virtue of the notification of President under Article 341(1 ) that the Scheduled Castes come into being . The members of the Scheduled Castes are drawn from castes , races or tribes , they attain a new status by virtue of Presidential Order . Clause ( 2 ) of Article 341 empowers Parliament alone by law to include or exclude from the list of Scheduled Castes specified in a notification issued under clause ( 1 ) by the President . By no executive power . amendment , modification , alteration or variance _ in _ the Presidential Order is permissible . It is not open to the executive to do anything directly or indirectly which may lead to any change in the Presidential Order .

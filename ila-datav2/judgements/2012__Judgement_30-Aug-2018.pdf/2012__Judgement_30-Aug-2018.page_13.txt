Part XVI of the Constitution of India deals with special provisions relating to certain classes . Article 330 provides for reservation of seats for Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes in the House of the People ( Lok Sabha ) whereas Article 332 contains similar provisions so far as the Legislative Assemblies of the States are concerned .

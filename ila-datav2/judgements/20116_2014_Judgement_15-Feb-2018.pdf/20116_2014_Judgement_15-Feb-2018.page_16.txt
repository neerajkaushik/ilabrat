It is , thus , the enforcement mechanism , which is akin to the enforcement of a decree but the award itself is not a decree of the civil court as no decree whatsoever is passed by the civil court . It is the arbitral tribunal , which renders an award and the tribunal does not have the power of execution of a decree . For the purposes of execution of a decree the award is to be enforced in the same manner as if it was a decree under the said Code . 16 .

This is the reason why the Union of India while inviting applications for recruitment considers all candidates , including reserved candidates on all India basis . Group B Cadre of DANICS / DANIPS is the Feeder Cadre for IAS and IPS respectively ( i.e. AIS ) . They retire invariably in these Offices , some of them reaching high positions in the Central Government . Recruitment to AIS ; CCS Group A ; CCS Group B ( Gazetted ) is conducted through UPSC . In the Civil Services Examination , the applicants are common when the applications are made . Every aspirant seeks recruitment to the services and it is only as per the marks and ranking that allocations are made eventually to AIS ; CCS Group A ; CCS Group B. Therefore , when UPSC undertakes the recruitment , it is naturally a pan India 72

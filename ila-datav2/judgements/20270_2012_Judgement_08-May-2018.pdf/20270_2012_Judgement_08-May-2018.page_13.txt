( 1A ) The Central Government may in consultation with the State Government , reserve any area not already held under any prospecting licence or mining lease , for undertaking prospecting or mining operations through a Government company or corporation owned or controlled by it , and where it proposes to do so , it shall , by notification in the Official Gazette , specify the boundaries of such area and the mineral or minerals in respect of which such area will be reserved . ] ( 2 ) The State Government may , with the approval of the Central Government , reserve any area not already held under any prospecting licence or mining lease , for undertaking prospecting or mining operations through a Government company or corporation owned or controlled by it and where it proposes to do so , it shall , by notification in the Official Gazette , specify the boundaries of such area and the mineral or minerals in respect of which such areas will be reserved .

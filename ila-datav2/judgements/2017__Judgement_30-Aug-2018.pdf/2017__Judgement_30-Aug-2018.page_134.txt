Marri Chandra Shekhar Rao and Action Committee are applicable to the States and they are applicable with equal force to the Union Territories including Union Territory of Delhi . There can not be any distinction between the States and the Union Territories . Likewise , there can be no distinction between Union Territory of Delhi and other Union Territories . When Presidential Orders of Scheduled Castes / Scheduled Tribes are notified for various Union Territories including Union Territory of Delhi extending PAN India reservation to the employment falling under the services of Union Territories including Union Territory of Delhi , will be against the Constitutional scheme and the law laid down in Marri Chandra Shekhar Rao and _ Action Committee .

This appeal is filed by the appellant who has been convicted for offences under Sections 302 , 376(2)(f ) and 342 of the Indian Penal Code ( for short , ‘ IPC ' ) . He was awarded death penalty for the offence punishable under Section 302 , IPC by the trial court vide its judgment dated September 27 , 2013 in Sessions Case No . 87 of 2012 . For offence under Section 376(2)(f ) of IPC , the appellant was sentenced to life imprisonment and for the offence under Section 342 of IPC , the trial court awarded simple imprisonment for two months .

The government must have freedom of contract . In other words , a fair play in the joints is a necessary concomitant for an administrative body functioning in an administrative sphere or a quasi - administrative sphere . However , the decision must not only be tested by the application of the Wednesbury principle of reasonableness , but must also be free from arbitrariness and not affected by bias or actuated by mala fides .

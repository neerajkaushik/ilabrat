It is a case where the findings of conviction are concurrent in nature and based on appreciation of evidence , therefore , such findings are usually binding on this Court . However , if the appellant is able to show any perversity , arbitrariness , absurdity or illegality in any such concurrent findings then , in such circumstances , the findings though concurrent are not binding on this Court . This Court , therefore , usually does not take upon itself to again appreciate the evidence de novo third time in the appeal subject to the exception pointed out above . 9 .

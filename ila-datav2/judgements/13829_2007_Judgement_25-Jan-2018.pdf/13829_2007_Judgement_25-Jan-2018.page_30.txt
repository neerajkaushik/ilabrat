One is “ Salus Populi Supremo Lex ” which means the safety of the people is the supreme law and the other is " Salus republicae supremo lex ' which means safety of the State is the supreme law . 39 ) In our considered view , it is the prime duty , rather statutory duty , of the Police personal / administration of every State to maintain and give precedence to the safety and the morality of the people and the State . Indeed , both are important and lie at the heart of the doctrine that the welfare of an individual must yield to that of the community . The Act and the Order 2005 are enacted keeping in view the safety and the morality of the people at large .

Per incuriam is a Latin term which means “ through lack of care ” or through inadvertence ( of a judicial decision ) wrongly decided , mainly because the judges were ill - informed about the applicable law . The word “ incuria’literally means “ carelessness ” . A decision is renderedper incuriamif it is made through some mistake , or under a misapprehension as to a decision or a dictum of a judge , which is the result of a material oversight . A decision , judgment or verdict can be renderedper incuriamif given without considering any provisionin a statute which was not brought to the notice of the court or if it is not possible to reconcile its ratio with that of a previously pronounced judgment of a co - equal or larger bench ; or if the decision of a High Court is not in consonance with the views of the Supreme Court .

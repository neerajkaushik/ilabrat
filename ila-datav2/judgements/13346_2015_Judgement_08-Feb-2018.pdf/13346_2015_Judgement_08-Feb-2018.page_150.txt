Income Tax Officer , Calcutta ( 1980 ) 2 SCC 191 , held that no one can suffer from the act of the court and in case an interim order has been passed and petitioner takes advantage thereof and ultimately petition is found to be without merit and is dismissed , the interest of justice requires that any undeserved or unfair advantage gained by a party invoking the jurisdiction of the court must be neutralized . 131(d ) . This Court in Mahadeo Savilaram Shelke v. Pune Municipal Corporation ( 1995 ) 3 SCC 33 has observed that the court can under inherent jurisdiction ‘ ex debito justitiae ’ has a duty to mitigate the damage suffered by the defendants by the act of the court .

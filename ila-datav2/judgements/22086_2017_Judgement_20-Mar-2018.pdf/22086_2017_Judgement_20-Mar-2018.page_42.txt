The Magistrate while authorising detention of the accused shall peruse the report furnished by the police officer in terms aforesaid and only after recording its satisfaction , the Magistrate will authorise detention ; 11.5 . The decision not to arrest an accused , be forwarded to the Magistrate within two weeks from the date of the institution of the case with a copy to the Magistrate which may be extended by the Superintendent of Police of the district for the reasons to be recorded in writing ; 11.6 . Notice of appearance in terms of Section 41-A CrPC be served on the accused within two weeks from the date of institution of the case , which may be extended by the Superintendent of Police of the district for the reasons to be recorded in writing ; 11.7 . Failure to comply with the directions aforesaid shall apart from rendering the police officers concerned liable for departmental action , they shall also be liable to be punished for contempt of court to be instituted before the High Court having territorial jurisdiction . 11.8 .

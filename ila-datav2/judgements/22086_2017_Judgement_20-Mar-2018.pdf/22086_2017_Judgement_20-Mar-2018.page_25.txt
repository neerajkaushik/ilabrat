The very same fundamental question arises in the facts and circumstances of this case also , viz . , ‘ whether any statute like the present Atrocities Act , especially enacted for the purposes of protecting weaker sections of the society hailing from S.C. & S.T. communities can be permitted to be abused by conveniently converting the same into a weapon of wrecking personal vengeance on the opponents ? ’ The answer to this question is undoubtedly and obviously ' No ' .

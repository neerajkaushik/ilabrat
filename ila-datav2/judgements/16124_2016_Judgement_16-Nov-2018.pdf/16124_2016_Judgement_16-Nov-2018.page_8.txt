Before we examine the factual matrix of the case at hand , it is apposite to take note of the provisions of the Act , which have relevance while deciding the claim petition . 16 . At the outset , we may reiterate as has been consistently said by this Court in a series of cases that the Act is a beneficial piece of legislation enacted to give solace to the victims of the motor accident who suffer bodily injury or die untimely . The Act is designed in a manner , which relieves the victims from ensuring strict compliance provided in law , which are otherwise applicable to the suits and other proceedings while prosecuting the claim petition filed under the Act for claiming compensation for the loss sustained by them in the accident .

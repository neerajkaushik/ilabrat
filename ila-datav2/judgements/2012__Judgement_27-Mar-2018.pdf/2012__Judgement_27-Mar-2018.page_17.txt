As rightly observed by the High Court , Anilkumar Patel has gone to the extent of even disputing his signature in the award dated 07.07.1996 by drafting choreographed petition . Having accepted the award through Anilkumar Patel , being the head of the family , appellant Nos . 1(a ) to 1(d ) and respondent No.10 can not turn round and contend that they had not received the copy of the award .

Advocates alone entitled to practise . -Except as otherwise provided in this Act or in any other law for the time being in force , no person shall , on or after the appointed day , be entitled to practice in any Court or before any authority or person unless he is enrolled as an advocate under this Act . 35   . Punishment of advocates _ for misconduct - ( 1 ) Where on receipt of a complaint or otherwise a State Bar Council has reason to believe that any advocate on its roll has been guilty of professional or other misconduct , it shall refer the case for disposal to its disciplinary committee . ( 1-A ) The State Bar Council may , either of its own motion or on application made to it by any person interested , withdraw a proceeding pending before its disciplinary committee and direct the inquiry to be made by any other disciplinary committee of that State Bar Council . ( 2 ) The disciplinary committee of a State Bar Council [ * * * ] shall fix a date for the hearing of the case and shall cause a notice thereof to be given to the advocate concerned and to the Advocate - General of the State .
